Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025. This policy explains how MindEase collects, uses and protects your personal information.

1. Who We Are

MindEase (www.mindease.app) is a free mental health education platform founded by Abdul Ghani. We provide educational content on mental health topics and are not a medical service provider.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide voluntarily

  • Name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Name, email and message content when you contact us via our contact form

Information collected automatically

  • Browser type and version, operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search query
  • General geographic location (country/city level only)

We do not collect sensitive personal data, financial information, or any data that could identify your mental health status.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you the newsletter you subscribed to
  • To respond to your contact form submissions
  • To understand how our website is used and improve our content
  • To maintain website security and prevent abuse

4. Cookies

We use only essential cookies for:

  • Storing your theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Basic analytics (page views, session duration) — fully anonymized

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party cookies for marketing purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. We may share anonymized, aggregated usage data to improve our services.

6. Data Retention

Newsletter subscriber data is retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months then deleted. Analytics data is retained for 26 months in anonymized form.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and similar data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@mindease.app.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. Our website is served over HTTPS. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

MindEase is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.

11. Contact

For privacy enquiries: privacy@mindease.app | Contact form
